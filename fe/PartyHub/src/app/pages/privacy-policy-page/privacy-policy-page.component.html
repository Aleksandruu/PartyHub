Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how we, PartuHub, collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when using our ticket selling application.

Information Collected

To provide our services, we may collect the following types of personal information:
Identification information, such as name, email address, and date of birth.
Payment information, such as credit/debit card number and expiration date.
Purchase information, including details of purchased tickets and transaction history.

Use of Information

The personal information collected will primarily be used for:
Processing and delivering purchased tickets.
Managing your account and providing customer support.
Communicating with you regarding our services, including special offers and promotions.

Protection of Information

We are committed to protecting the personal information we collect. We use appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of personal information.

Sharing of Information

The personal information collected will not be sold, exchanged, or shared with third parties except where necessary to provide the services requested by you, such as payment processing.

Access and Update of Information

You have the right to access, correct, update, or delete the personal information we have collected about you. You can do this through your account or by contacting us directly.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will take effect immediately upon their publication in the application. If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
